🔐 Permission Configuration Required

What's happening?

Your Microsoft account has been authenticated successfully, but the application doesn't have the necessary permissions to access your emails and calendar. This commonly happens when:

  • Initial consent was not completed properly
  • Your account is an external user with restricted permissions
  • The application permissions need admin approval

💡 Solution

Try these steps to resolve the permission issues:

  1. Force Re-consent: This will take you through the Microsoft consent process again to ensure all permissions are properly granted.

    🔄 Re-authorize with Full Permissions
  2. Check Azure Portal: If you're an admin, verify that the application permissions are properly configured:
    • Mail.Read, Mail.ReadWrite
    • Calendars.ReadWrite
    • User.Read

    🔧 Open Azure Portal
  3. Contact Administrator: If you're not an admin, you may need to ask your organization's administrator to grant consent for this application.
